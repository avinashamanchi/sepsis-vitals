The Problem

How It Works
Three steps. Zero lab work.
From bedside vitals to actionable alerts in under a minute.

1. Enter Vitals

Nurse enters temperature, heart rate, respiratory rate, blood pressure, SpO2, and GCS at the bedside. Works on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop.

2. Instant Scoring

Five validated algorithms score the patient simultaneously. Each vital is benchmarked against CDC NHANES population data for the patient's age, sex, and ethnicity.

3. Smart Alerts

High-risk patients trigger real-time alerts with AI-generated risk narratives. The system recommends next steps and escalation paths based on clinical guidelines.
