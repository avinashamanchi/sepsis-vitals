# Clinical Validation Protocol Draft

## Study Design

Prospective observational validation at two partner hospitals before scaling to
five sites.

## Stages

1. Silent mode: model runs locally but no alerts are shown.
2. Alert-only mode: nurses see risk tier, top driver, and last reading time.
3. Interventional escalation phase: only after ethics amendment and site
   clinical governance approval.

## Primary Endpoint

Sensitivity and specificity for prospective sepsis cases at the pre-specified
operating threshold.

## Secondary Endpoints

- AUROC.
- AUPRC.
- Time-to-antibiotic.
- Mortality.
- Alert rate per 100 encounters.
- Alert acknowledgement rate.
- Escalation rate.
- Nurse false-positive feedback rate.

## Workflow

The V1 nurse screen must show only:

- Patient name or local ID.
- Low, medium, or high risk.
- Top contributing factor.
- Time of last reading.
- Acknowledge.
- Escalate.

No raw probability is shown in V1.
