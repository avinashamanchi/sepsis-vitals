FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about getting started.

How does per-bed pricing work?

You are billed based on the number of active monitored beds in your facility, not total hospital capacity. A "bed" is any patient station actively streaming vitals to Sepsis Vitals. If you have 200 total beds but only monitor 80 through our system, you pay for 80. Bed counts are measured as a rolling monthly maximum, so occasional spikes during high-census periods are handled gracefully.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every new hospital gets a full 30-day trial with complete Professional-tier access. No credit card required. During the trial you get unlimited beds, AI-powered risk narratives, NHANES benchmarking, advanced analytics, and priority support. At the end of the trial, you can choose the plan that fits your needs or continue with a limited free tier.

What about HIPAA compliance?

Sepsis Vitals is fully HIPAA compliant across all tiers. We employ AES-256-GCM encryption for all protected health information (PHI) at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit, and maintain comprehensive audit trails for every data access event. Enterprise customers receive a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA). We undergo annual SOC 2 Type II audits and maintain continuous compliance monitoring. All infrastructure is hosted on HIPAA-eligible AWS services.

Can we integrate with our EHR?

Absolutely. Our Enterprise tier includes full EHR integration via the HL7 FHIR R4 standard. We have pre-built connectors for Epic, Cerner (Oracle Health), MEDITECH, and Allscripts. Integration typically involves mapping vital signs data feeds and configuring alert routing. Our dedicated integration team handles the setup process end-to-end. Professional tier customers can use our REST API for custom integrations.

What if we need custom scoring algorithms?

Custom scoring algorithms are available on our Enterprise tier. You can work with our clinical engineering team to define custom scoring criteria, thresholds, and alert logic specific to your patient population or institutional protocols. We also support importing validated scoring models from your own research teams. Custom algorithms undergo the same rigorous validation pipeline as our built-in scores.

How long does implementation take?

A typical implementation takes about 2 weeks from contract signing to go-live. Week 1 covers technical setup: connecting vital signs data feeds, configuring alert thresholds, and setting up user accounts. Week 2 focuses on staff training and a supervised pilot period. Enterprise deployments with EHR integration may take 4-6 weeks. Our fastest deployment was 3 days for a cloud-hosted Starter configuration.

Is training included?

Yes, training is included for Professional and Enterprise tiers. Professional customers receive virtual onboarding sessions for clinical staff and administrators, along with access to our on-demand training library. Enterprise customers get in-person training at your facility, custom training materials branded to your hospital, and ongoing education sessions for new hires. Starter customers have access to our self-service documentation and community forum.

What happens to our data if we cancel?

If you cancel your subscription, you have a 90-day data export window during which you can download all of your data in standard formats (CSV, HL7 FHIR JSON, or PDF reports). During this period, your dashboard remains in read-only mode. After 90 days, all data is securely and permanently deleted in accordance with our data retention policy and HIPAA requirements. We will never hold your data hostage or charge for data export.
